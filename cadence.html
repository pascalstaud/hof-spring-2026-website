Cadence

The architectural expression. Monochrome — precise, not brutal. Full dark, exterior and interior, in a single disciplined palette.

Cadence — the precise point where rhythm becomes structure. Dark charcoal and gunmetal meet deep emerald accents, inspired by precision watchmaking and sculptural black forms. Hexagonal textures on the seats recall the geometry of engineered surfaces. The flat-bottom steering wheel grounds the experience in driver's intent.

Restriction doesn't limit expression. It intensifies it. Every element in its correct place. Nothing added. Nothing removed. The pulse beneath the collection.

Where Prelude opens, Cadence holds. The tension that gives the bloom its meaning.

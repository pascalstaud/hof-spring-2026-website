PRELUDE.

Prelude is not a season. It's a shift. Light returns. Colours soften. Structure gives way to movement. Something held in tension begins to open.

HOF composed this moment into an object. Not the bloom — the state before it.

HOF G · V — Prelude · Collector Model · SS26

Shaped by Couture.

Every surface is a deliberate act. Two tones — warm champagne and a deeper ground — in a satin finish that holds light, softens it, lets it go.

Inside, the atmosphere shifts. Textile inserts carry a botanical jacquard created for Prelude alone — a floral language drawn from the Kenzo couture tradition, reinterpreted in grey and ivory.

The cabin is a room. Composed, not configured.

Exterior — Satin finish, two-tone composition.

Interior — Botanical textile inserts, warm leather.

Detail — Every stitch placed by hand, Sindelfingen.

A Composition You Inhabit.

Presence. Not the kind that announces — the kind you notice once you're inside. A mood. A material. A silence.

"True elegance appears at the moment something opens, not when it insists."

The most intimate composition. Outside: composed, almost neutral — a silver-white metallic that catches light with crystalline precision, accented only by fine amber pinstripes.

Inside: deep amber. Like fossilised resin held against a window. Two interior worlds exist within one composition: bold monochrome amber, and the inverted expression where white leather meets targeted amber accents.

FAQ

How do I order a HOF composition? ⌄
Every HOF begins with a personal consultation. Contact us to book an appointment at our atelier in Sindelfingen, or request a private consultation at a location of your choice.
Can I choose between the two interior expressions? ⌄
Yes. Genese offers two curated interior worlds: the full monochrome amber, and the inverted white-with-amber expression. Both are part of the same composition.
What platforms are available? ⌄
HOF compositions are currently available on the Mercedes G-Class platform in three derivatives: G 5-Door, G 3-Door Coupé, and G 3-Door Cabriolet.

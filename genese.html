Genese

The most intimate composition. Outside: composed, almost neutral — a silver-white metallic that catches light with crystalline precision, accented only by fine amber pinstripes. Inside: deep amber. Like fossilised resin held against a window. Like the last hour of light in a west-facing room.

Genese — the origin point. Where something begins to glow. Inspired by the translucency of natural amber, the mechanical precision of haute horlogerie, and the warm canyon landscapes where earth meets light.

Two interior worlds exist within one composition: the bold monochrome amber — every surface saturated in a single conviction — and the inverted expression, where white leather meets targeted amber accents at the speaker surround, steering wheel, and headliner.

Outside, restraint. Inside, conviction.

KINSŌ.

Performance Model · SS26

TOKYO.

Light made solid. Cast crystal resin — amber-orange, transparent, alive — embedded across the interior like frozen fire. Inspired by Edo Kiriko, Tokyo's glass-cutting tradition since 1834. Two expressions: total amber immersion or white gallery with amber art.

Seasonal Model · SS26

NOTAN.

Two tones. One conversation. The Japanese art of light-dark harmony, made physical. Karbonium — colored carbon fiber, beyond standard black — replaces every trim element. Four two-tone compositions, each a dialogue between temperatures.

MA.

The beauty of what remains when everything unnecessary is removed. Bouclé woven textile wraps every surface. Metallic leather that looks like brushed metal but feels like skin. One composition. No options. A living room on wheels.

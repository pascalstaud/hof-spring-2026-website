Naming Reference
Kinsō (金装) — Japanese for “golden attire.” The art of adorning the surface to reveal the soul within.
Tokyo (東京) — Eastern Capital. The city that never stops refining.
Notan (濃淡) — Japanese for “light-dark harmony.” The interplay of intensity and restraint.
Ma (間) — Japanese for “negative space, the pause between.”
Sir Class — Tribute to Sir Lewis Hamilton. Permanent model, evolved from AW25.
Sir Class 1063 — 1,063 PS tribute to the 7× World Champion. Sold out.

Prelude

Prelude names both the season and its defining composition. Collection and object share one name — the strongest statement in the HOF system.

The reference: early botanical illustration. Precision of line. Patience of observation. The art of recording something alive before it changes.

From there: a satin exterior in a two-tone early-morning palette — champagne meeting indigo in a finish that holds light like a petal holds dew. An interior of botanical textile inserts drawn from the tradition of Parisian couture and hand-placed embroidery. Warm leather chosen for what it'll become.

Every detail calibrated to one moment: the state before bloom.

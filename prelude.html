Prelude

Prelude names both the season and its defining composition. The reference: early botanical illustration. Precision of line. Patience of observation.

A satin exterior in a two-tone early-morning palette — champagne meeting indigo. An interior of botanical textile inserts drawn from the tradition of Parisian couture and hand-placed embroidery.

Craft Sheet

Exterior ⌄
Satin two-tone finish in a botanical palette. Champagne meeting indigo. Discreet HOF identification. A presence that arrives without announcement.
Interior ⌄
Botanical jacquard textile inserts, created exclusively for Prelude. A floral language drawn from the Kenzo couture tradition, reinterpreted in grey and ivory.
Embroidery ⌄
Hand-placed botanical motif, visible only on close inspection. A private language for those who look.
Leather ⌄
Warm palette, vegetable-tanned, selected for patina. Chosen for what it will become, not what it is today.
Wheels ⌄
Disc-style, white with gold ring accent. Completing the composition's tonal harmony.
Atelier ⌄
Completed by hand at Atelier HOF, Sindelfingen. Edition: Limited · SS26 · Collector Model.

FAQ

How do I order a HOF composition? ⌄
Every HOF begins with a personal consultation. Contact us to book an appointment at our atelier in Sindelfingen, or request a private consultation at a location of your choice.
What is the delivery timeline? ⌄
Each composition is completed by hand. Timelines vary by model and specification. Your personal consultant will provide detailed scheduling during the consultation process.
Can I customise a seasonal composition? ⌄
Seasonal compositions are curated as complete expressions. For bespoke requirements beyond the collection, our Meet the Designer programme offers fully individual compositions.
What platforms are available? ⌄
HOF compositions are currently available on the Mercedes G-Class platform in three derivatives: G 5-Door, G 3-Door Coupé, and G 3-Door Cabriolet.

FAQ

How do I order a HOF composition? ⌄
Every HOF begins with a personal consultation. Contact us to book an appointment at our atelier in Sindelfingen, or request a private consultation at a location of your choice.
What is the delivery timeline? ⌄
Each composition is completed by hand. Timelines vary by model and specification. Your personal consultant will provide detailed scheduling during the consultation process.
Can I customise a seasonal composition? ⌄
Seasonal compositions are curated as complete expressions. For bespoke requirements beyond the collection, our Meet the Designer programme offers fully individual compositions.
What platforms are available? ⌄
HOF compositions are currently available on the Mercedes G-Class platform in three derivatives: G 5-Door, G 3-Door Coupé, and G 3-Door Cabriolet.

Design Philosophy
Core Concept Botanical illustration meets Parisian couture. Precision of line, patience of observation. Duality and inversion. One exterior, two interior worlds. Single thought, two readings. Architectural expression. Restriction intensifies expression. Monochrome conviction. Form follows feeling. Beauty lives in the unhurried. Minimal expression, maximum presence. Evolution of a legend. What began as tribute now stands permanent. Tribute to motorsport's most decorated champion. Final lap made forever.
